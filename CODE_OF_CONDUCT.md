# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This project is a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We are here to build reliable tooling together, in good faith.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Welcome newcomers and differing viewpoints; critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent; give and accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language or imagery.
- Personal or political attacks, or sustained disruption of discussion.
- Publishing others' private information without their consent.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer at **st.chiotis94@gmail.com** or via [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/stylianos-chiotis/). Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove contributions and may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban anyone who violates this code.
